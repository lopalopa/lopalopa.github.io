Sample MCQs

1. Which of the following is not a nitrogenous base found in DNA?

  • A. Uracil
  • B. Adenine
  • C. Guanine
  • D. Cytosine

Answer: A. Uracil

2. The structure of DNA was proposed by?

  • A. Watson & Crick
  • B. Hershey & Chase
  • C. Rosalind Franklin
  • D. Linus Pauling

Answer: A. Watson & Crick
